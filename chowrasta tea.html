🌿 Chowrasta Signature Tea 🌿

“The Four Roads Chai”

Inspired by the word “Chowrasta” — meaning “four roads” — this one-of-a-kind blend brings together four iconic Indian tea cultures into a single cup of flavorful harmony.

  • Darjeeling Base: Light, floral, and fragrant.
  • Assam Boldness: Strong, malty undertones for depth.
  • Kashmiri Kahwa Touch: Hints of saffron, almonds, and cardamom.
  • South Indian Masala: Infused with warming spices like ginger and cinnamon.

Served in traditional clay kulhads, paired with a jaggery-coated biscuit. Optional add-ons: rose petals 🌹 or tulsi infusion 🌿.
